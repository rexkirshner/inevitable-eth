## Definition

The idea is to separate a specific object with the generalized concept. In computer science, we call this principle abstraction.Â 

An object might be this specific apple: ğŸ, but the abstraction might be all apples or even all fruits.Â 

Another way to look at abstraction is from the perspective of the user.Â 

Let's think about price bot from above. Price bot will take in the basic information about the fruit (type, quality, weight, etc) and return a price.Â 

From the user's perspective its simple: give inputs, get output.Â 

Take a look behind the scenes and you'll realize price bot is incredibly sophisticated. Regression models, price scraping, Monte Carlo forecasting, etc.Â 

Yet the user never has to worry about any of it.Â 

Abstraction is the concept that developers should simplify how they interface with their users as much as possible. Developers building on top of your work can access your functionality without becoming a master in your code.Â
